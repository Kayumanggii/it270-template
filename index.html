Hello! My name is CLARA

Seattle born, Philippine rasied (Camiguin Island to be exact). Jesus lover, Whiskey drinker, Dog + Cat mama, and 2000's Hiphop and RnB kind of gal.

I was a preschool teacher in my past life and loved everything about it. I had to make a decision to switch careers since teaching was unfortunately not paying my Seattle living bills. I transitioned temporarily into working in the healthcare field while figuring out adulting and what sparked joy in my life.

So, I decided to go back to school. I have always loved design and decided on pursing an Associate in Applied Science in Web Design. I have been having so much fun learning throughout my classes and super excited to be done by the end of Fall 2020 Quarter. Woot woot!
